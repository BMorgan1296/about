About Me

I am a PhD candidate at The University of Adelaide and a computer security researcher at DST Group.

My research interests revolve around the intersection between hardware and software security concerning microprocessors. In a nutshell, this work involves reverse engineering processors to exploit flaws in their design, construction, and behaviour, to breach security boundaries and potentially retrieve data not meant to be observed.

As a researcher, my aim is to ensure experimental repeatability of my work, rigorous and honest reporting of results, as well as effective yet concise scientific communication. If you don't see a code link below, rest assured it is on the way!
